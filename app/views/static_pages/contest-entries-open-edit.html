Edit Brief

Submit your best Street-Styled Denim Coat for men

Participate

Upload a design of your most awesome street-style denim coat. Preferable in a live environment. You can wear it yourself or let a model be wearing your products.

Creative Brief

Chic, clever, luxurious and attainable, London-based luxury handbag brand, Milli Millu is a breath of fresh air in the handbag industry. Milli Millu believes any woman can be a great stylist, so they thought, why not have their customers and fans, alike, share their styling ideas for inspiration?

Target Audience

Milli Millu invites fashion designers from around the world to create a street-chic, styled look for the chance.

What is required

This amazing opportunity for fashion designers could help you get your foot in the door of the fashion industry so if you have a flair for style and an eye for fashion, Milli Millu would love to see and share your ideas by submitting a photograph of yourself in your proposed outfit.

Style theme ideas for inspiration

What to avoid

Guidelines

Designers should submit full body photographs in color in JPEG or PNG (5MB Max) featuring their preferred look for the competition.The design on the photograph must be the artist's own original work, meaning not using third party material. The selected designer has the option to commute to Milli Millu’s studio in London, UK at their own expense to participate in the Styling Ideas Photo Shoot. Designers must join using an active email address so if selected, they can be easily contacted. Wear My Tags reserves the right to disqualify participating artists who we believe are cheating the voting system as this is against the spirit of the Creative Invite. All submissions will be featured in digital galleries to promote your entry via public voting on Facebook and Twitter. Voting will give you additional exposure and recognition for your work as well as new fans and supporters. Inappropriate or harmful submissions will be removed. No purchase necessary.
