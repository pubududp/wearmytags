Step 1/4

Write design brief

It all begins with an awesome design brief to get the designers attention. Tell designers what you want in your fashion design. Answer the questions in our simple online brief with as much detail as you can.

Step 2/4

Launch contest and receive design from all over the world!

Your design brief is submitted to our contest list. Skilled fashion designers will take it from here. From New York to Paris and Caracas. All of our designers will see if they can help you!

Give feedback and pick your finalists!

Improve your incoming designs by providing with detailed feedback. Let the designers know what you’re looking for. After a set periode you can pick your finalists...
