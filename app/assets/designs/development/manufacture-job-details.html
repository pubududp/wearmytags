Manufacture a playsuit for Jessie J with glacéau

Creative Brief

Chic, clever, luxurious and attainable, London-based luxury handbag brand, Milli Millu is a breath of fresh air in the handbag industry. Milli Millu believes any woman can be a great stylist, so they thought, why not have their customers and fans, alike, share their styling ideas for inspiration?

Milli Millu invites fashion designers from around the world to create a street-chic, styled look for the chance to be featured in Milli Millu’s social media outlets, as well as in Milli Millu's newsletter that goes to over 15,000 subscribers.

This amazing opportunity for fashion designers could help you get your foot in the door of the fashion industry so if you have a flair for style and an eye for fashion, Milli Millu would love to see and share your ideas by submitting a photograph of yourself in your proposed outfit.

Fashion Designers are welcome to take inspiration from Milli Millu’s Stylings Ideas section on the company website; we are looking for fashion forward yet stylish looks.
